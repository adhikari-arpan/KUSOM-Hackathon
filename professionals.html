Greatest IT Professionals from Nepal

Mr. Shailendra Raj Giri

Mr. Shailendra Raj Giri is an eminent figure in the Nepalese Human Resource (HR) industry with more than 18 years of experience in the areas of strategic HRM, Organizational Culture & Environment, Talent Acquisition & Management, Career Development, Continuous Feedback for Performance, Compensation and Benefits. Mr. Giri has been engaged as the Managing Director to Real Solutions, Merojob, Rojgari Services and Aayulogic with a common motto to empower the Nepalese HR industry in creating an impact over the national economy.

Dr. Subarna Shakya

Dr. Subarna Shakya is a distinguished professor in Information Technology and Computer Engineering. With extensive research in network security and e-governance, he has significantly contributed to the development of IT infrastructure in Nepal.

Manish Jha

Manish Jha is the co-founder and CEO of F1Soft International, one of the leading fintech companies in Nepal. Under his leadership, F1Soft has developed numerous innovative financial products and services that have transformed the banking and financial industry in Nepal.

Mahabir Pun

Mahabir Pun is a renowned Nepali scientist, teacher, and social entrepreneur known for his extensive work in developing the Nepal Wireless Networking Project, which has brought internet access to remote areas of Nepal. His efforts have significantly contributed to the IT and education sectors in the country.

Rabi Karmacharya

Rabi Karmacharya is an IT entrepreneur and social activist who founded Open Learning Exchange Nepal. His work focuses on using technology to improve education in rural Nepal, providing digital learning materials to schools and communities.

Dr Suresh Manandhar

Prof. Suresh Manandhar is an Artificial Engineering Consultant to the Board. He has a B.Tech. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (India), M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence from University of Essex (UK) and PhD in Artificial Intelligence from University of Edinburgh (UK). He is is an AI scientist with over 30 years in the field with interest in machine learning and natural language processing. He headed AI research group at University of York until 2019, where he successfully supervised over 20 doctoral students and published over 120 papers.
